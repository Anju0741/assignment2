Explore Jammu & Kashmir

About Jammu & Kashmir

Jammu and Kashmir, known as "Paradise on Earth," is a land of mesmerizing natural beauty, majestic snow-capped mountains, serene lakes, and vibrant culture. From the bustling bazaars of Srinagar to the tranquil valleys of Gulmarg, Jammu and Kashmir offer unforgettable experiences for every traveler.

Top Attractions

Dal Lake

Enjoy a serene Shikara ride on the iconic Dal Lake, surrounded by stunning mountain views.

Gulmarg

Visit Gulmarg, a paradise for skiing and home to the world-famous Gulmarg Gondola.

Pahalgam

Explore the lush valleys of Pahalgam, perfect for trekking, camping, and river rafting.
