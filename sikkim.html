Welcome to Sikkim

The Land of Peace, Tranquility, and the Majestic Himalayas

Why Visit Sikkim?

Sikkim, nestled in the lap of the mighty Himalayas, is known for its peaceful environment, lush green valleys, monasteries, and breathtaking views of the Kangchenjunga. A perfect destination for nature lovers and those seeking spiritual solace.

Top Attractions in Sikkim

Tsomgo Lake

A serene high-altitude lake surrounded by snow-capped mountains, perfect for a peaceful retreat.

Rumtek Monastery

One of the largest and most significant Buddhist monasteries in Sikkim, offering spiritual peace.

Kangchenjunga

The third-highest peak in the world, offering stunning views and challenging treks for adventure seekers.

Sikkim's Culture

Sikkim is a cultural blend of Buddhist monasteries, traditional Nepali heritage, and a wide variety of rituals and festivals. The state celebrates various festivals like Losar (Tibetan New Year), and Buddha Jayanti with great fervor and devotion.
