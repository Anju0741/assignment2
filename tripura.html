Welcome to Tripura

The Land of Culture, Heritage, and Natural Beauty

Why Visit Tripura?

Tripura, located in the northeastern part of India, is a land of diverse culture, lush green landscapes, and ancient temples. With its vibrant festivals, royal palaces, and serene lakes, Tripura is a hidden gem for travelers seeking beauty and tranquility.

Top Attractions in Tripura

Ujjayanta Palace

An architectural marvel that was once the royal residence of the Tripura Maharajas, now a museum with rich historical exhibits.

Rudrasagar Lake

A picturesque lake, perfect for boating and relaxation amidst nature’s calmness.

Neermahal Palace

The stunning water palace on Rudrasagar Lake, showcasing the regal lifestyle of the Tripura kings.

Tripura's Culture

Tripura has a rich cultural heritage, with influences from both Indian and neighboring Southeast Asian cultures. The state celebrates numerous festivals like Kharchi Puja and Durga Puja, which are steeped in tradition and local customs.

Adventure in Tripura

Adventure seekers can enjoy trekking, wildlife safaris in Tripura’s wildlife sanctuaries, and birdwatching in its lush forests. The state’s pristine beauty offers a perfect retreat for explorers and nature enthusiasts.
