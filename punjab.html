Top Attractions in Punjab

Golden Temple

The holiest shrine of Sikhism, known for its peaceful atmosphere and stunning architecture.

Jallianwala Bagh

A historic site that commemorates the tragic massacre during British rule in 1919.

Anandpur Sahib

A revered Sikh pilgrimage site known for its religious significance and festivals.

Punjabi Cuisine

Indulge in rich and flavorful Punjabi dishes such as Butter Chicken, Sarson Da Saag, and Makki Di Roti. Punjabi cuisine is known for its hearty and spicy flavors.

Festivals of Punjab

Punjab celebrates vibrant festivals like Baisakhi, Lohri, and Diwali. These festivals are marked by music, dance, and traditional rituals.
